Technology of Biometric Systems:

Biometric technology is the analysis of specific human traits such as fingerprints, DNA, voice pattern, eye retinas and irises and hand measurements. Biometric systems keep advancing as technology continues to progress. Biometric technology appears in a variety of forms such as: fingerprint, voice patterns, palm printing and iris scanning. Due to the advancement in technologies, the iris printing has been discovered to be the most accurate type of biometric technology as they have discovered that as one ages their fingerprint changes, therefor leading to a possible inaccurate identification.
